About GEOPOLIS

Academic credibility with institutional continuity

GEOPOLIS Society is a student-led, institutionally anchored collective within the Department of Politics & International Studies, Pondicherry University, guided by a formally codified governance structure, rigorous editorial standards, and a structured cohort-based leadership transition.
